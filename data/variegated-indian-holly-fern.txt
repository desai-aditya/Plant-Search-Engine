This beautiful fern from Japan  and China features shiny, dark green fronds, erect and deeply cut, with a yellow stripe down the center of each segment. It forms  a clump 1018 inches tall and twice  as wide. Evergreen in the Lower and Coastal South; deciduous in the Middle and Upper South, where it emerges late in spring. Naturalized in some parts of the Southeast. Showy addition to the shade garden.
