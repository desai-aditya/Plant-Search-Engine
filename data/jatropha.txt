Large group of tropical and subtropical plants related to poinsettia (), with milky latex or clear juice in the stems. The most commonly grown species are popular for their large, deeply lobed, typically heart-shaped leaves, which provide tropical effects in the garden.Need gritty, very well-drained soil with added humus; not salt tolerant. Resist deer. Indoors, site in bright light, but don't expose to direct hot sun. In spring and summer, water moderately and feed monthly with a general-purpose liquid houseplant fertilizer. Leave plants unwatered in fall and winter.
