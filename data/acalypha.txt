All three species described here are native to Southeast Asia  and the Pacific Islands. Beyond their hardiness range, they can  be grown indoors; species  Acalypha wilkesiana and Acalypha pendula can be used as annuals in cold-winter areas. All bloom intermittently during the warm months and  all must have good drainage. Pinch young plants regularly to encourage bushy growth.
