Native to the mountains of Europe. Attractive, creeping perennial, nice for ground  cover in shaded rock garden or woodland planting; evergreen  in mild-winter areas. Medium green leaves are oval, pointed,  24 inches long; form a mat about  3 inches high, spreading to 3 feet or more. Half-inch-wide spring flowers are an intense pure blue with a tiny white eye. Tolerates drought but looks better with regular water.
