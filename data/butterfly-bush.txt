Colorful spring or summer flowers, sweet fragrance, attractiveness to butterflies, and easy care make these shrubs extremely popular. The vast majority sold are selections of Buddleia davidii, but many other species are highly ornamental and deserve their fair share of attention. Most types prefer full sun and fertile, moist, well-drained soil. Not browsed by deer. Removing spent flowers extends bloom.Nanho series. Plants grow to 46 feet tall and wide, with small, narrow, silvery leaves. 'Nanho Alba' is dense, with white flowers in 8- to 10 inches spikes. 'Nanho Blue' offers profuse blue blooms in 4- to 6 inches spikes. 'Nanho Purple' has dark violet flowers in 4- to 6 inches spikes.
