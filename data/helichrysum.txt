Best known are the annual strawflowers (Helichrysum bracteatum and Helichrysum subulifolium) used in fresh and dried arrangements. Others, though less familiar, are choice plants for landscape use. Seldom bothered by deer.For shrubby perennial forms (such as 'Cockatoo', 'Dargan Hill Monarch', 'Diamond Head') previously included with annual strawflower, see Bracteantha bracteata.
